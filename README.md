## 📅 Exam Format Details

### Question Types
- **Multiple choice** — Select one or more correct answers
- **Case studies** — A scenario followed by several related questions
- **Drag and drop / hot area** — Match items or click the correct location in a diagram
- **Performance-based lab** — Live Azure environment tasks (approximately 12 sub-tasks)

### Scoring
- Score range: 1–1000
- Passing score: **700**
- Not all questions are weighted equally; some are experimental and unscored
- The performance-based lab is scored separately and contributes significantly to the total

### Time Management Tips
- You have **150 minutes** total
- Allocate approximately **2–3 minutes per standard question**
- The performance-based lab can take **30–45 minutes**; complete it first or last, not in the middle
- You can flag and review questions before submitting
